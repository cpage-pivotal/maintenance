SECTION 5 — AD COMPLIANCE TRACKING REQUIREMENTS

All Airworthiness Directives applicable to Airplane-100 series aircraft must be
tracked at the tail-number level. Operators must maintain compliance records
cross-referenced against:
  - FAA AD number
  - Effective date
  - Compliance method (one-time inspection, recurring, terminating action)
  - Last compliance date and next due (in FH and calendar)

For recurring ADs, the compliance management system must generate a pre-alert
at 90% of the compliance interval and a hard alert at 100%.
